The Alpha

Jasinda Wilder's erotic romance novel Alpha (2014) follows a young woman who attracts the attention of a rich and mysterious man when she can't afford to pay her bills, but who then decides that it's time that she pays back her debt to him. The first book in the Alpha series, it won the 2015 Audie Award for Erotica.
